Simple Sabotage Field Manual 簡單破壞行動戰地手冊
====================================================

**Simple Sabotage Field Manual** is an old manual on *simple sabotage* (sabotage
that involves minimal danger of injury, detection, and reprisal) written by the
US Office of Strategic Services (precursor to the Central Intelligence Agency) 
in 1944.

**簡單破壞行動戰地手冊** 乃係美國戰略情報局（中央情報局前身）於1944年編寫嘅手冊，
內容講述 *簡單破壞行動* （即相對低受傷、被偵測、及引致反擊等風險嘅破壞行動）嘅實
踐概略。

The manual has been declassified in 2008.

該手冊於2008年解密。
